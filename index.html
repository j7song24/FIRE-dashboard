Key Assumptions

  • Tax filing: Married filing jointly; 2025 federal MFJ brackets, CA state brackets, FICA (6.2% SS up to $168,600 + 1.45% Medicare + 0.9% additional Medicare over $250K combined); standard deduction $30,000
  • RSUs: Vest on a 4-year schedule with 1-year cliff, sold at vest (ordinary income); grants grow at salary growth rate
  • Side income: Active = 15.3% SE tax on 92.35% of earnings (half deductible from AGI); Passive = no SE tax
  • Salary & contributions: Cash salary and RSU grants grow at the salary growth rate; 401k/IRA limits grow with inflation (rounded to nearest $1,000); employer match capped at min(match% × cash salary, 401k limit)
  • Housing: 30-year fixed mortgage; property tax 1.1%; insurance 0.35% of home value; down payment deducted from portfolio in the purchase year
  • Kids: Costs vary by age — daycare (0-4), K-8, high school (14-17), university (18-21), done at 22; spaced 2.5 years apart; “with-kids bump” adds extra lifestyle expenses while any child is at home
  • Expenses: Base other expenses grow at inflation rate; with-kids bump applies only while kids are at home (ages 0-21)
  • FIRE number: Perpetual base expenses (housing + other + healthcare) / SWR / (1 − tax) + PV of remaining kids costs (including lifestyle bump) grossed up for withdrawal taxes
  • Portfolio: Grows at real (inflation-adjusted) return; post-FIRE healthcare ($2,500/mo default) included in withdrawal needs
  • Quit at FIRE mode: Income drops to zero at FIRE age; portfolio funds all expenses via withdrawals grossed up for taxes
